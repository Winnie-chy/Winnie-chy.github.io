Reunderstanding Health

With the development of the times, people’s material life has become more abundant, and the quality of life has risen rapidly all over the world. Therefore, more and more people hope to have a longer lifetime, so people generally concentrate more on the topic about health.

Due to the Career Experience Day organized by the school after CIE exam, I had the opportunity to visit and experience Cooper Aerobics Company. It was the first time for me to have this experience and enter the health field that I have never known. The car took us into the mountains, where our destination was hidden in: Cooper’s Aerobics. When we walk through the door, the staff did not rush to let us start the visit, first she let us have some understanding of the real “health”. Cooper Aerobics has eight rules for maintaining health which are:
1.Take a regular, comprehensive physical exam
2.Exercise 5 days a week
3.Maintain a healthy weight
4.Make healthy food choices most of the time
5.Take the right supplements for you
6.Control alcohol
7.Don’t smoke/ Use tobacco
8.Manage your stress

I thought the Career Experience Day was always just an all-day-long lecture, but to my surprise, we actually started exercising in the morning. Fifteen of us divided into two groups, completed tests of flexibility (sit and reach), strength (push-ups) and endurance (one-mile race walk) to see how old we were in terms of physical fitness. I have no exercise for a long time, so I deeply felt the necessity of regular exercise. Lunch was not quite what I had expected, either. Eating less is not the right way to stay healthy and every meal should be full. The ratio of meat, vegetables and staple food must be 1:1:2. The dishes there were low calorie meals with less oil and salt than normal restaurant, which are delicious and healthy. In the afternoon, we still carried on the exercise, this time is the mental and physical coexist small games which were different from the morning. Through the two games, the staff there led us to understand the importance of teamwork. Only with a unified goal, a clear division of labor and strict observance of the rules can the task be completed quickly and well. Our team narrowly beat the odds and won a mug for each of us.

When I got home, I searched the Internet for more information about Cooper Aerobics Company. Its founder, Dr. Kenneth Cooper, is the personal doctor of former President George W. Bush, the world’s “father of aerobics”, a pioneer in the field of sports and public health around the world, and a master of “curing disease” in Western medicine. In the 1960s, Dr. Cooper discovered the relationship between aerobic exercise and health while doing physical testing for American astronauts in NASA. He invented the word “Aerobics” and published the best-selling book Aerobics which swept the world in 1968, profoundly affecting the world. He changed people’s old ideas and health conditions and opened a revolution in fitness exercise that people all over the world love – aerobic exercise. Dr. Kenneth Cooper was therefore honored as the “Father of Aerobic Exercise” in the world. (From: http://www.cooperaerobics.cn )

In conclusion, I don’t think people should lose weight blindly and regard thinness as beauty. Healthy is the most important thing that everyone should pay attention to. Follow Cooper’s eight health rules and have regular check-ups, I’m sure all of us will become healthier and healthier.
