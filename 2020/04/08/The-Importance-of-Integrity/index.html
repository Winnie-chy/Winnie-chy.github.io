The Importance of Integrity

Recently the news about Luckin successfully caught the public’s eye. When this star company revealed its own financial fraud of 2.2 billion yuan, a run on the bank and a burst of stock was inevitable. A lot of people must have seen the news and feared luckin would bankrupt, so they wanted to use up their coupons before it did. That was the reason why orders flooded in which was crazy and sudden.

The interesting thing is, Luckin has overtaken Starbucks, which has been operating in China for 20 years in total in less than two and a half years.

For ordinary consumers, thoughts about luckin depends more on how long the coupons last. A survey on Weibo asked, “Will you still support luckin?”. Of the total 198,000 people who voted, 100,000 of them said they would drink it if they didn’t go out of business, while another 88,000 said they’d never had love or use up their coupons. Moreover, there have been questions about luckin’s taste, marketing, pricing and management’s true motives. The news occurred on April 3 run act as a signal to the market that a company might be able to spend money at scale but will not cultivate brand loyalty.

In short, luckin has grown to an amazing scale in a very short period of time, gaining a lot of customers almost by letting them enjoy lower prices than other stores and giving away coupons. It is true that the speed of development is shocking, but the disadvantage is that it cannot really attract fixed customers. I believe that once luckin’s price rises, most people will not choose luckin. Why? Because luckin’s quality is not enough to make customers feel it is worth the price, they will forget about luckin and choose other products. This is because luckin has no brand loyalty.

Affected by the outbreak, all industries have suffered great losses, luckin is no exception. Under such circumstances, luckin can only cheat to increase sales in order to gain the trust of investors. But sooner or later this will expose the loophole, completely lost people’s trust.

In fact, there are a lot of companies like Lukin in China, through this incident, I realized that integrity is the most important. For companies, making quality products is more effective than sending coupons. Also, for individuals, remember always be honest to everyone. Otherwise, your lies will be exposed one day.
